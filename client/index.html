Instructions

The rules of the game are simple - Score on the other team!

Log in by typing in a user name and pressing submit. When you log in, you are assigned a team - the left paddle or the right paddle. You control this paddle and move it up by pressing "W", the up arrow on your keyboard, or the up-button below the pong board. You move your paddle down by pressing "S", the down arrow on your keyboard, or the down button below the pong board.

Here is the catch, though - for your paddle to move, everyone on your team must be in unison! In other words, for your paddle to move up, everyone on your team must be attempting to move the paddle up! Below the pong board, you can see who is on your team and what button they are pressing.

If you wish to only watch and not be on a team, check the "spectator" box before hitting submit. Happy ponging!
